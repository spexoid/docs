Refund/Cancellation Policy

Last updated on Apr 7th 2023

Refunds are only applicable under these scenario:


  • Accidental initial payment
  • Accidental double payment
  • Breach of contract by SPEXOID TECH PRIVATE LIMITED
  • Cancellation of service before the work is started

Refund request will not be considered valid under these scenario:


  • Breach of contract by client's side
  • Cancellation of services after the work is started
  • After the work is finished and is in compliance with the contract
  • Extended support payment will not be refunded unless there is accidental initial or double payment.

Request regarding cancellation and refund can be sent to mail@spexoid.com.

The refund will be processed within 3-5 business days.

Documentation by SPEXOID.COM. you can contact us by calling +91 9012 107 108. or by mailing at mail@spexoid.com.
